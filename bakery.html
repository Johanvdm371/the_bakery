It all starts before dawn. As the ovens start up and the dough begins to prove, the smells of fresh bread and pastries fill the air:
This is the smell of love!

The Bakery is a family affair. Since gran decided to share her beautiful baked bounty with her neighbours all those years ago we've been commited to sharing her love of all things baked with the world.
Come and share in that love!

Fresh from the oven

Every morning we fire up those ovens to create the most amazing fresh baked goods. Nothing beats that fresh bread smell early in the morning and that first bite of warm dough. Our bakers bring with them the knowledge and experience needed to create only the best. We don't sell it if it isn't fresh and amazing!

Made to oder

Special orders are baked fresh just before pickup. Whether you need fresh bread, pastries, cakes, or a bit of everything, we'll make it as fresh as can be to order. No time to pick up your order? We'll bring it to you! Simply ask about our delivery options when ordering.
